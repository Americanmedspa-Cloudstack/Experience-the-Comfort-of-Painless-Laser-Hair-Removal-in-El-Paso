What to Expect Before, During, and After Treatment

Before Your Session:

  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning beds for at least two weeks.
  • Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment.
  • Refrain from waxing, plucking, or using depilatory creams leading up to the session.
  • Avoid skincare products that contain retinol or acids that could increase skin sensitivity.

During Your Session:

  • A cooling gel or cooling device will be applied to prepare the skin.
  • The laser will be adjusted based on your skin and hair type for precise results.
  • Quick pulses of light will target the hair follicles with minimal discomfort.
  • The procedure will be completed quickly, depending on the treated area.

After Your Session:

  • Mild redness or sensitivity may occur but typically fades within a few hours.
  • Avoid direct sun exposure and apply sunscreen regularly.
  • Follow any aftercare instructions provided by your specialist.
  • Expect hair to gradually shed over the following weeks, leading to smoother skin.

How does laser hair removal work?

During laser hair removal, a laser emits a gentle beam of light over the area where you want the hair removed. The light energy is then absorbed by the pigment within the hair, which is converted into heat, effectively destroying the hair follicle and avoiding damage to the surrounding skin.

The results are long-lasting, preventing any future hair growth. Once a hair follicle is destroyed, it cannot regenerate or produce new hair … ever!
